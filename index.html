💸 The 8th Wonder of the World

Einstein (reportedly) called compound interest "the eighth wonder of the world." A $1,000 investment at 22 vs 32 makes a $100k+ difference by retirement. Learn why on the Finance page.

🧠 You're Not As Rational As You Think

You have 200+ cognitive biases shaping every decision you make — from what you buy to who you trust. Most are invisible. Psychology page breaks down the biggest ones.

😴 Sleep Is Your Superpower

Sleeping less than 7 hours increases your risk of obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease. One night of poor sleep reduces decision-making ability by 40%. Health page explains the science.

🏛️ Ancient Wisdom, Modern Problems

Stoic philosophers 2,000 years ago developed frameworks for handling anxiety, uncertainty, and loss that modern therapy still draws from. Philosophy page shows you how to apply them today.

🌍 Geography Shapes Everything

Why is Europe rich and Sub-Saharan Africa poor? Why do rivers determine where cities exist? Geography doesn't just tell you where things are — it explains why the world is the way it is.

🧩 Rubik's Cube ≠ Just a Toy

Solving a Rubik's Cube builds algorithmic thinking, spatial reasoning, and working memory. Learn which puzzles to give kids at which ages — and what each one actually develops cognitively.
